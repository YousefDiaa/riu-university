About Rabbanian International University

Rabbanian International University, abbreviated as (RIU EDU), is an Islamic university operating through distance learning. Its main administrative headquarters is in the United States, licensed under number (21000012381), and registered with the U.S. Federal Government and the Department of Labor.

The university offers higher education at various levels (Diploma, Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctorate) and issues certificates authenticated by a U.S. notary public, the U.S. Department of State, and attested by several embassies in the United States.

Study Systems

We have designed several study systems to suit the circumstances and needs of students around the world. Currently, we offer three study systems.

1 Live

Live Study

Students attend lectures via Telegram, Zoom, or Google Meet alongside the course instructor. Attendance is mandatory; absence results in grade deductions.

Grade Distribution

Attendance 20 points
Assignments 20 points
Final Exam 60 points
Total 100 points
2 Self-Paced

Self-Study

Students receive the course materials and are required to summarize the content. Attendance is not required in this system.

Grade Distribution

Summary 40 points
Final Exam 60 points
Total 100 points
No attendance required
3 Flexible

Affiliation

Students are provided with the required university books and study plan. Attendance and summarization of books are not required.

Grade Distribution

Final Exam 100 points
Total 100 points
No attendance required
No summary required
